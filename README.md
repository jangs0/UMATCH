# uniply
A web platform that matches international students to best-fit US universities by grant odds, and profile.
We analyse, not just filter. Other platforms give you filters and a list. UMatch analyses your exact profile — grades, budget, citizenship and more — and matches it to colleges that truly fit you.
Built for international students. We account for your citizenship and need-blind status — the things that actually decide your chances. Most tools don't.
Real grant probability. Not just "is it affordable" — your actual chance of accepting and funding, based on who you are.
